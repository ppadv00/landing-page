Are these programs truly free?

Most programs offer digital certificates or badges upon successful completion. Certificate requirements vary by program—some are automatic, while others require passing assessments or completing specific milestones.

Programs range from self-paced modules taking a few hours to structured courses spanning several weeks. Each program listing includes estimated duration to help you plan accordingly.
Can I access these programs from any country?
Most programs are globally accessible. However, some may have regional restrictions or language availability limitations. Check individual program details for specific requirements.
